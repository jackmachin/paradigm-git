Welcome to Target

Featured in this Month’s Issue

In this month’s edition of Target we provide important details of yet more change to pension rules that firms need to comply with. We also provide background information in relation to forthcoming changes to data protection law that firms should start to consider planning for now. We follow up last month’s article with an update regarding P2P, highlight the need to comply with new companies house requirements in the shape of the new PSC register and provide an overview of the FCA business plan and areas of focus for the regulator over the next twelve months.
